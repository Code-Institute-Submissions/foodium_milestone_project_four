About us

Our Story

Situated in Farum Midpunt , Vasi and Chef Tikelo Tre celebrated Foodium features Copenhagen contemporary cuisine. Foodium serves a nightly-changing tasting menu highlighting the restaurant's love for luxury taste

Accolades

Chef Tikelo Tre was named “Best Chef: Pacific” by the James Beard Foundation in 2020. Foodium was awarded its first Michelin star in 2019 and holds four stars from the Copenhagen rating. Foodium is a proud member of Relais & Châteaux, an association of the world's most celebrated restaurants and luxury hotels
